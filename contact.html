Q: How accurate is your water calculator?
A: Our calculator uses scientifically-backed formulas (35ml per kg of body weight) adjusted for activity levels, which are widely accepted by nutritionists and health professionals.

Q: Is my personal information stored?
A: No! All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store your weight, activity level, or any personal data on our servers.

Q: Can I use this if I have health conditions?
A: Our calculator provides general guidelines. If you have medical conditions, are pregnant, or take medications that affect hydration, please consult your healthcare provider.
