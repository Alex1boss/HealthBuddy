üí° Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are your calculators?
A: Our water calculator uses the widely accepted 32.5ml/kg formula, while our drinks calculator follows FDA caffeine guidelines (400mg daily limit). Both are based on peer-reviewed research and clinical recommendations.

Q: Why do caffeine calculations matter?
A: Caffeine has mild diuretic effects, reducing net hydration. Our calculator accounts for this (~10ml loss per 100mg caffeine) to give you accurate hydration totals and remaining water needs.

Q: Is my data stored or shared?
A: Absolutely not! All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't store weight, caffeine intake, or any personal data on our servers.

Q: Can I use this during pregnancy?
A: Our calculator includes pregnancy mode (200mg caffeine limit), but please consult your healthcare provider for personalized hydration and caffeine advice during pregnancy.

Q: What about children or teens?
A: Our calculators are designed for adults. Children and teens have different hydration and caffeine tolerance needs - please consult a pediatrician for guidance.
