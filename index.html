How Much Water Should You Drink Daily?

Our water intake calculator helps you determine the right amount of water for your body and lifestyle. Based on scientific recommendations, the calculator considers your weight and activity level to provide personalized hydration goals.

Water Intake Calculation Examples:

70kg person, low activity:
Daily water goal: 2.1 liters (8.5 cups)
70kg person, active:
Daily water goal: 2.8 liters (11.2 cups)
80kg person, moderate activity:
Daily water goal: 2.6 liters (10.4 cups)

Quick Formula: The basic calculation is 30-35ml per kilogram of body weight, with additional water needed for exercise and hot weather. Our calculator automatically adjusts for your activity level to give you the most accurate daily water intake recommendation.
